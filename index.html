INSTRUCTIONS



This is AI PING PONG, you use your mouse to move the paddle up and down, you want to block the ball from passing you! You need to win four times to win! But the computer is tough competitor to play with. Move your mouse up and down to move the paddle up and down. Also the speed goes higher and higher the more you play in a rally. Good luck and I hope you have fun playing!
